A Rigorous Mental Health Assessment Built for Founders

This assessment combines six validated clinical instruments into a single 10-minute screening designed specifically for venture-backed founders. It is, to our knowledge, the first of its kind.

Your results are benchmarked against a cohort of 847 founders across stages from pre-seed to Series C. You will receive immediate, personalized results with clinical context and recommended next steps.

Methodology
Instruments PHQ-9 (depression), GAD-7 (anxiety), ASRS-v1.1 (ADHD), AQ-10 (autism spectrum), SD3 (dark triad), Founder Stressors Index
Cohort 847 venture-backed founders, pre-seed through Series C, median age 34
Duration Approximately 10 minutes
Created by Keegan Walden and Jason Shen — Y Combinator alumni, executive coaches, and mental health researchers
